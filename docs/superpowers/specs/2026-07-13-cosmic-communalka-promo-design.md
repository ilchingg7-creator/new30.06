# Promo materials: Cosmic Communalka

## Goal

Create the required Yandex Games promotional artwork for **Cosmic Communalka**: a 512×512 icon, an 800×470 cover, and three desktop screenshots. The set must feel cohesive with the game's existing hand-drawn retro-futurist station rooms.

## Visual direction

- Warm, lived-in orbital communal home rather than sleek sci-fi.
- Hand-drawn 2D illustration, soft grain and slightly worn metal/plaster surfaces.
- Palette: warm cream walls, muted blue-grey machinery, amber practical lights, deep navy space.
- No watermarks, UI chrome, third-party logos, or generated typography.

## Assets

### Icon — 512×512 PNG

An immediately readable round porthole from an old space station: a warm amber inner rim, stars and blue nebula beyond the glass, with a small domestic detail such as a hanging lamp or plant. Use no text; the silhouette must work at small sizes.

### Cover — 800×470 PNG

Use advertising key art rather than a scene that reads as an in-game screenshot: a large luminous porthole establishes an immediate space hook, while a compact lived-in communal station fills the foreground. Include warm domestic details—such as a kettle, plaid throw, a small cat, plants and lamps—so the contrast between home and space is clear at thumbnail size. The image needs one dominant focal point, bolder contrast and a clean area for later title treatment outside the generated image.

### Desktop screenshots — three PNGs

1. **Cosmo kitchen:** the developed shared kitchen, pantry shelves and a warm meal-preparation atmosphere.
2. **Oxygen garden:** a thriving hydroponic wall with soft growth lamps, utility pipes and plants.
3. **Zero-G laundry:** compact washed-metal room with portholes, a laundry machine and small floating household details.

## Production and validation

- Generate each asset independently using the built-in image generator.
- Save source artwork under `promo/` with descriptive names.
- Downscale/crop copies to the exact console dimensions where necessary while preserving PNG output.
- Visually review every resulting PNG for legibility, cohesive palette, absence of text artifacts and dimensions before upload.
- Uploading the final files to Yandex Games and saving the draft require user confirmation at action time.
