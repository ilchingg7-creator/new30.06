# Content Roadmap

## Purpose

This document records future content ideas without expanding MVP scope. MVP stays focused on 8 modules, 6 residents, 10-15 goals and one prestige loop. Roadmap content should guide extensible ids, save schema, station hardpoints and Pixi scene composition.

## MVP Boundary

MVP includes only:

- 8 station modules from `02-economy-balance.md`;
- 6 residents from `03-content-progression.md`;
- 10-15 goals;
- one station visual line from old module to first orbit renovation;
- rewarded bonuses for income, offline reward and VIP resident.

Roadmap content must not be required for first publication.

## Content Pillars

### Modules

New modules should add one clear fantasy, one visible station silhouette and one economy role.

Future module candidates:

| Module | Fantasy | Economy Role | Visual Hook |
|---|---|---|---|
| Radiator Balcony | Residents drink tea near a warm radiator in space | comfort multiplier | glowing radiator fins |
| Mail Tube Office | Pneumatic mail through station walls | goal rewards | tube network and mail capsule |
| Meteorite Pantry | Stores strange snacks and emergency soup | offline income | armored pantry door |
| Shared Observatory | Residents argue about constellations | reputation gain | small telescope dome |
| Repair Brigade Garage | Keeps old modules humming | cost discount | tool bay and blinking van |
| Gravity Shoe Rack | Stores shoes that refuse to stay down | resident unlocks | magnetic shoe wall |
| Comet Water Tank | Collects icy comet water | comfort and service income | blue tank and frost |
| Orbital Library | Quiet room with floating books | prestige upgrades | book window and lamp |

### Residents

Residents should remain readable, friendly and bonus-focused. Each resident needs:

- one unlock condition;
- one short bonus;
- one memorable prop;
- one line of cozy-comedy flavor.

Future resident candidates:

| Resident | Prop | Unlock Direction | Bonus Direction |
|---|---|---|---|
| Retired Cosmonaut Neighbor | enamel mug | first prestige | starting income |
| Three-Eyed Housekeeper | key ring | comfort threshold | cheaper early modules |
| Comet Plumber | wrench and scarf | repair module | offline cap |
| Signal Radio Host | headphones | observatory | bonus duration |
| Floating Librarian | book strap | library | goal rewards |
| Tiny Saucer Family | luggage | saucer dock | resident collection bonus |

### Goals

Goals should guide the player through one next useful action. Avoid broad goals that do not teach a system.

Future goal categories:

- module level milestones;
- unlock first resident in a category;
- reach comfort threshold;
- use a voluntary bonus once;
- perform prestige;
- rebuild faster after prestige;
- unlock a cosmetic station change.

### Cosmetics

Cosmetics should not change power in MVP. Later they can become long-term rewards.

Future cosmetics:

- station nameplate styles;
- window light colors within style guide limits;
- corridor sticker packs;
- antenna shapes;
- resident room decorations;
- prestige exterior paint layers.

## Pixi Scene Planning

The Pixi station scene should reserve future hardpoints without rendering locked roadmap modules as active content. Recommended zones:

- central living spine for MVP modules;
- upper arc for comfort/service modules;
- lower arc for logistics modules;
- outer ring for prestige-era modules;
- small decorative sockets for cosmetics.

Hardpoints should use stable ids so future modules can be added without reworking save data or scene layout.

## Content Release Order

Recommended after MVP:

1. More goals and first-day retention content.
2. Two new residents tied to first prestige.
3. Two new modules that extend the station silhouette.
4. Cosmetic nameplates and window variants.
5. First weekly event.
6. More prestige upgrades.

## Rejection Rules

Do not add content if it:

- requires a new economy layer before the first MVP is validated;
- cannot be represented clearly on 390x844;
- forces long tutorial text;
- breaks cozy-comedy tone;
- makes rewarded ads mandatory;
- requires final art rights that are not cleared.
